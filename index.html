Your New Floor in 4 Simple Steps

Our meticulous process ensures a flawless, permanent bond and a stunning finish every time.

1

Diamond Grinding

We begin by mechanically grinding your floor to remove imperfections and open the concrete's pores for a permanent bond.

2

Base Coat & Flakes

We apply a tinted polyaspartic base coat, then a full broadcast of your chosen decorative vinyl flakes for color and texture.

3

Scrape & Vacuum

Once cured, we scrape the floor to create a smooth, even texture, then vacuum away all debris for a clean surface.

4

Clear Top Coat

A crystal-clear, UV-stable polyaspartic top coat locks everything in, providing ultimate protection and a glossy finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are some of the most common things we get asked.

Most residential garage and basement projects are completed in just one day. You can walk on the surface in a few hours and drive on it within 24 hours.

We provide a convenient storage solution! We'll deliver a trailer to your driveway a few days before we begin your project. You can store all of your garage items in the trailer during the coating process. This service is available for $100 per day.

There is no comparison. Our system uses industrial-grade polyaspartic materials and professional diamond-grinding preparation. This ensures a bond that will not peel or fail, unlike DIY kits which often use inferior epoxy and rely on acid etching, which is a far less effective preparation method.

The vinyl flakes provide natural texture, creating a surface that is not slippery. For areas that may get wet frequently, we can include an anti-slip aggregate in the top coat for added grip, tailored to your needs.
