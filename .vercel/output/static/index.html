Your New Floor in 4 Simple Steps

Our meticulous process ensures a flawless, permanent bond and a stunning finish every time.

1

Diamond Grinding

We begin by mechanically grinding your floor to remove imperfections and open the concrete's pores for a permanent bond.

2

Base Coat & Flakes

We apply a tinted epoxy base coat, then a full broadcast of your chosen decorative vinyl flakes for color and texture.

3

Scrape & Vacuum

Once cured, we scrape the floor to create a smooth, even texture, then vacuum away all debris for a clean surface.

4

Clear Top Coat

A crystal-clear, UV-stable polyaspartic top coat locks everything in, providing ultimate protection and a glossy finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are some of the most common things we get asked.

A professional garage floor coating system using epoxy base, decorative flakes, and a polyaspartic top coat in Massachusetts ranges from $8 to $12 per square foot, depending on factors like garage size, surface condition (e.g., need for repairs or moisture barriers), and material quality.

For a standard 2-car garage (approximately 400-500 square feet), expect to pay $3,000 to $6,000 total. This includes surface preparation, the epoxy base layer, full broadcast of decorative flakes for texture and aesthetics, and a durable polyaspartic clear top coat. This assumes a basic installation without major extras.

Most residential garage floor coating and basement floor coating projects are typically completed within 1-3 days, depending on project size and complexity. Commercial projects may take longer based on square footage and facility requirements. You can walk on the surface within 24 hours after installation, and the coating is fully cured for vehicles within a few days depending on conditions. Learn more about our installation process.

We provide a convenient storage solution! We'll deliver a trailer to your driveway a few days before we begin your project. You can store all of your garage items in the trailer during the coating process. This service is available for $100 per day.

Professional installation makes a significant difference. Our system uses industrial-grade materials and professional diamond-grinding preparation, which ensures a permanent bond that will not peel or fail. DIY kits often rely on acid etching, which is a far less effective preparation method. We offer both epoxy and polyaspartic optionsÃÂpolyaspartic provides superior durability and faster curing, while epoxy offers a cost-effective solution. Either way, professional installation guarantees superior results compared to DIY approaches. Explore our professional coating services.

The vinyl flakes provide natural texture, creating a surface that is not slippery. For areas that may get wet frequently, we can include an anti-slip aggregate in the top coat for added grip, tailored to your needs.

It's incredibly easy. The non-porous surface means spills wipe right up. For general cleaning, a simple pH-neutral cleaner and a mop or squeegee are all you need. Harsh chemicals are not necessary.
